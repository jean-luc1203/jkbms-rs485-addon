## Why this model?

The goal is to keep the project accessible to private Home Assistant users while protecting the work from commercial copying.

Private users should be able to monitor their own batteries without being blocked.

At the same time, a company, installer or reseller should not be able to take the work, especially the Premium dashboard system, and sell it as part of their own commercial offer without supporting or licensing the project.

## Community add-on

The community add-on is source-available for private, personal and non-commercial use.

It may be used by hobbyists, makers, private Home Assistant users and non-commercial users.

Commercial use requires written authorization.

## Premium dashboards

Premium dashboards are optional.

They add a professional, ready-to-use interface and help support the development of the project.

Premium dashboards are not open-source assets and may not be redistributed, resold or reused commercially without written authorization.

## Commercial use

Commercial use includes any use by or for:

- customer deployments

Commercial use requires a commercial license.
